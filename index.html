About Whole In One

Our Beginning

In 2023, 2 friends named Krish Roy and Vignesh Anand were playing their favorite sport golf. This is when they started craving some donuts, their favorite snack. Sadly, there were no donuts that were close to where they golfed. THey wanted to fix this issue by opening a store near their golf course the using their baking skills to use. THis eventually blew up and now they have 3 stores around the USA!
